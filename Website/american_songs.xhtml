The Songs of Colonization - American

About the Songs

The songs that make up this Corpus were all selected from Cowboy Songs and Other Frontier Ballads,1 which was first published in 1910. We choose this source because it was easily accessible and was already digitalized on the Project Guttenberg site. We selected songs whose content dealt directly with either expansion and possession of land or with settlersâ€™ relationships with native peoples. You will notice that this corpus is significantly larger than the German songs, this is due to multiple historical and practical reasons. First, we had a much easier time finding and accessing American folk songs online. The American period of expansion was also much longer and has been celebrated in popular culture to this day. This accounts for a greater public interest in these songs, which likely contributed to higher rates of publication and circulation. Due to the time and resource restrictions of this project, we embraced this disparity and went forward with a greater amount of American songs.
